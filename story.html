STORY

 
 

I saw a girl about many years ago. She was pretty and sweet. When I see her for the first time, I feel like I have fallen in love with someone for the first time. I have never seen such a beautiful girl in my life. I had a lot to tell her but I never told her my words, I tried to tell her but I couldn't. I thought one day she would realize my love but I was stupid about that. I hope I will tell her one day But now it's very difficult because I'm so far away from her now, I don't even know if I'll ever see her again and I hope she will be fine.

I still remember the previous moments when I was waiting on the porch, when I was standing on the porch to visit once, or just sitting in a tea shop and much more. The words are many years old but it seems to me that it happened a few days ago. I've never forgotten, I think I'll never forget in my life this all of a moment.

You know, I've never written to anyone like this before, this is my first time writing to someone. I'm sorry if I made a mistake in writing.

I just wanted to know some questions answer from her.

Why?

I am always waiting for your answer
